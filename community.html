Community Guidelines

Let's keep our community helpful, respectful, and welcoming for everyone

✅ Please Do

  • Share helpful housing tips and neighborhood insights
  • Ask questions - no question is too basic
  • Provide constructive feedback on RoomRadar
  • Be respectful of different backgrounds and experiences
  • Help newcomers feel welcome

❌ Please Don't

  • Post spam or promotional content unrelated to housing
  • Share personal information like phone numbers publicly
  • Be disrespectful or discriminatory toward others
  • Share potentially fraudulent housing listings
  • Flood the chat with repetitive messages
