Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RoomRadar's AI-powered student housing search platform.

Is RoomRadar free to use?

Yes! During our beta phase, RoomRadar is completely free for all users. We're focused on building the best possible experience and gathering feedback from students. Once we launch publicly, we'll maintain a free tier with premium features available.

Where do the listings come from?

Our AI scans public Facebook groups dedicated to student housing in Barcelona, as well as major rental platforms like Idealista, Fotocasa, and Badi. We only collect information that's already publicly available - we never access private data or require special permissions.

Why is RoomRadar only available in Barcelona?

We're starting with Barcelona because it has one of the most challenging student housing markets in Europe. The city's unique mix of local and international students, combined with high demand and limited supply, makes it the perfect testing ground for our AI. We plan to expand to other Spanish cities and European university towns soon.

How accurate are the AI trust ratings?

Our AI analyzes multiple factors including post completeness, photo quality, landlord communication patterns, and price consistency. While no system is perfect, our trust ratings have shown 85% accuracy in identifying legitimate listings. We continuously improve our algorithms based on user feedback and real-world results.

How do I get started with RoomRadar?

Simply join our beta by signing up on our homepage. You'll receive an email with your access credentials within 24 hours. Once logged in, you can set your preferences, budget, and preferred areas. Our AI will then start finding and rating listings that match your criteria.

Can I contact landlords directly through RoomRadar?

Yes! For each listing, we provide direct contact information including phone numbers, email addresses, and WhatsApp/Telegram contacts when available. We also include links to the original posts so you can see the full context and photos.

How often are new listings added?

Our AI scans for new listings every few hours, so you'll see fresh options throughout the day. You can also set up notifications to get instant alerts when new listings match your criteria, ensuring you're among the first to know about new opportunities.

What if I find a scam or fake listing?

We have a "Report Listing" feature on every listing page. When you report a suspicious listing, our team reviews it immediately and updates our AI algorithms to better detect similar scams in the future. Your feedback helps make RoomRadar safer for everyone.

Can I use RoomRadar if I'm not a student?

Currently, RoomRadar is designed specifically for students and young professionals looking for shared housing or student-friendly accommodations. However, many of the listings we find are suitable for anyone looking for affordable housing in Barcelona.

How can I provide feedback or suggest features?

We love feedback! You can join our Telegram community to share ideas, report bugs, or suggest new features. We also send regular surveys to beta users to understand what's working and what needs improvement. Your input directly shapes the future of RoomRadar.
