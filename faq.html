AI & Trust Ratings

Our AI trust ratings analyze multiple factors to assess listing reliability:

  • Content Quality: Detailed descriptions, clear photos, complete information
  • Landlord History: Previous posts, response patterns, verification status
  • Price Analysis: Comparison with market rates for similar properties
  • Red Flag Detection: Common scam indicators and suspicious patterns

While not 100% perfect, our ratings have shown 85%+ accuracy in beta testing for identifying trustworthy listings.

1 Star: High risk, likely scam or very poor quality
3 Stars: Average quality, some missing information
5 Stars: Excellent quality, verified landlord, complete information

Yes, AI systems can make mistakes, which is why we encourage users to:

  • Always verify information directly with landlords
  • Use our ratings as guidance, not absolute truth
  • Report any incorrect ratings to help improve our system
  • Apply common sense and trust your instincts

Our AI continuously learns from feedback to improve accuracy over time.
