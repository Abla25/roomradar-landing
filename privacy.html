Privacy Policy

At RoomRadar, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle data.

Introduction

At RoomRadar, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

This policy is effective as of January 15, 2025 and applies to all users of RoomRadar's services.

Who We Are

Service Name: RoomRadar

Purpose: Intelligent real estate listing aggregation system for student housing in Barcelona

Data Controller: RoomRadar Team

Contact Email: privacy@roomradar.app

What Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

Automated Collection

  • Public Facebook posts from rental groups
  • Images from public posts
  • Publicly available contact information (phone numbers, emails)
  • Rental information (prices, locations, descriptions)

Important Note:

We only collect data that is already publicly available on Facebook groups. We do not collect private or personal data beyond what is already public.

User-Provided Data

  • Email address (for beta access)
  • Housing preferences (budget, areas, room type)
  • Feedback and support requests

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Aggregate rental listings for users
  • Analyze and filter relevant housing posts
  • Provide reliability ratings for listings
  • Display rental information in an organized format
  • Send beta access credentials and updates
  • Improve our AI algorithms and service quality

Data Processing Legal Basis

Our data processing is based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: Providing housing search services to students
  • Public Data: Processing of publicly available information
  • Consent: For beta access and communications (explicitly given)

No consent is required for public data aggregation, but we always respect user preferences and provide opt-out options.

Data Protection Measures

We implement the following protection measures:

  • Automatic Censorship: Phone numbers are automatically censored
  • Email Protection: Email addresses are hidden from public view
  • Personal Identifiers: Personal identifiers are masked
  • Contact Anonymization: WhatsApp/Telegram contacts are anonymized
  • Secure Storage: All data is stored using industry-standard encryption

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Rights Related to Automated Decision Making: Request human review of automated decisions

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@roomradar.app

Data Retention

  • Listings: Automatically removed after 30 days
  • Cached Data: Regularly cleaned and updated
  • User Data: Retained until account deletion or 2 years of inactivity
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store personal data

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Notion: Database storage and management
  • OpenRouter/AI Services: Content analysis and AI processing
  • GitHub: Hosting and automation
  • Facebook RSS Feeds: Data source for listings
  • Tally.so: Beta signup form processing

All third-party services are GDPR-compliant and have appropriate data protection measures in place.

Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries:

  • Email: privacy@roomradar.app
  • Response Time: Within 30 days as required by GDPR
  • Data Protection Officer: Available upon request

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will notify users via email
  • Changes will be posted on this page
  • Effective date will be updated
  • Users will have 30 days to review changes

Cookie Policy

We use minimal cookies for essential functionality:

  • Session Cookies: For user authentication
  • Analytics Cookies: To improve our service (anonymized)
  • Preference Cookies: To remember user settings

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). We ensure:

  • Adequate data protection measures are in place
  • Standard contractual clauses are used where necessary
  • Compliance with GDPR requirements for international transfers

Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a data breach:

  • We will notify affected users within 72 hours
  • We will report to relevant authorities as required
  • We will take immediate steps to contain and resolve the breach
  • We will provide guidance on protective measures users can take

Complaints

If you have concerns about our data processing:

  1. First, contact us directly at privacy@roomradar.app
  2. We will respond within 30 days
  3. If unsatisfied, you can contact your local data protection authority

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.
