Introduction

At RoomRadar, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

We believe in clear, honest communication about data practices, especially when serving students who are navigating housing searches in Barcelona.

Who We Are

  • Service Name: RoomRadar
  • Purpose: Intelligent real estate listing aggregation system for Barcelona students
  • Data Controller: RoomRadar Team
  • Contact Email: privacy@roomradar.app

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at the email above. We are committed to responding within 30 days as required by GDPR.

What Data We Collect

Automated Collection from Public Sources

We collect the following information that is already publicly available:

  • Public Facebook posts from rental groups
  • Images from public posts
  • Publicly available contact information (phone numbers, emails)
  • Rental information (prices, locations, descriptions)
  • Public listing data from housing portals

Important Note

We only collect data that is already publicly available on Facebook groups and housing platforms. We do not collect private or personal data beyond what is already public.

User-Provided Information

When you sign up for our beta, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Housing preferences (budget, location, type)
  • University or study program information
  • Feedback and communication with our team

How We Use Your Data

We use collected data for the following purposes:

Public Data Processing

  • Aggregate rental listings for users
  • Analyze and filter relevant housing posts
  • Provide reliability ratings for listings
  • Display rental information in organized format

User Data Processing

  • Personalize housing recommendations
  • Send relevant listing notifications
  • Improve our AI algorithms
  • Provide customer support

Data Processing Legal Basis

Under GDPR, we process data based on the following legal grounds:

Legitimate Interest

We have a legitimate interest in providing housing search services to Barcelona students by processing publicly available information.

Public Information Processing

No consent is required for processing information that is already publicly available on social media and housing platforms.

Consent

For beta users, we process personal information based on explicit consent provided during registration.

Data Protection Measures

We implement several automatic protection measures to safeguard personal information:

Automatic Censorship

  • Phone numbers are automatically censored
  • Email addresses are hidden
  • Personal identifiers are masked
  • WhatsApp/Telegram contacts are anonymized

Additional Security

  • 🔒 Secure data encryption
  • 🛡️ Regular security audits
  • 🔄 Automatic data cleanup
  • ⚡ Minimal data retention

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all data we hold about you

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete information

Right to Erasure

"Right to be forgotten" - request deletion of your data

Right to Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a machine-readable format

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@roomradar.app. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

Please include sufficient information to verify your identity and specify which right you wish to exercise.
